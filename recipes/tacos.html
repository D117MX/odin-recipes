Tacos

Tacos Mexico's best food

Tacos is Mexico's most food around the globe from different meats and veggies there is truly a type of taco for everyone.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • meats
  • Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Peppers
  • Tomatillos
  • Salt
  • Limes

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Heat up tortillas
  3. Chopp Cilantro and Onions
  4. Grill or freshly blend the peppers and tomatillos to make salsa
  5. add salt to salsa and meat to liking
  6. Place meat on tortillas
  7. add onions and cilantro to tacos
  8. add salsa to your tacos
  9. squeeze lime to liking

ENJOY!
